Alert

Communicates a state resulting from a user's interaction with a feature.

We use alerts to communicate important information to users throughout their experience. Alerts should be clear and recognizable, but never interrupt a user's ability to interact with affected elements.

Welcome to BarkBox!

Your order was successful.

Your payment is processing.

Your payment was declined.
