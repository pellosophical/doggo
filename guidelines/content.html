Guidelines

Content

Before your write for BarkBox, it's important to think about our readers.

Though our voice doesn't change much, our tone adapts to our users' feelings.

Voice

The foundation for communicating BarkBox products and brand message. Our voice expresses the core of our personality, while bringing comfort, ease, and a sense of connection to our users.

Tone

Conveys your attitude toward what you are writing about and who you are writing to. When you create content – whether it's introductory text on the UI, an error message, or a topic in the docs – you are a storyteller.
