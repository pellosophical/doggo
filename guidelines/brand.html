Guidelines

Brand

At BarkBox, our goal is to make dogs and their parents happy. We are, literally, for the dogs.

Vision

We want to find people who love their dogs as much as we love ours and surprise and delight them each and every month. Dogs aren’t pets, they’re family and they deserve the best treats, toys, and seat on the couch. We’re driven to be the people our dogs think we are. Dog people get it.

Identity

As humans, we love good, clean design—but dogs love fun. We are always looking to find the balance between clean and playful.
