Style

Typography

Typography is the core structure of any well designed interface.

It is used to create clear hierarchies, useful organizations, and purposeful alignments that guide users through the experience.

Display

Equinox is a versatile & functional hand-drawn display typeface used to inject the Bark brand & character into large-scale sections like the homepage or marketing landing pages.

Head & copy

Apercu is a grotesque sans-serif typeface with character that distinguishes it from other grotesques. It comes in a variety of weights for establishing clear hierarchy in product design.

Scale

The typographic scale is designed to keep the number of separate styles to the minimum. We do not necessarily use all sizes, but in the event a new size is necessary, it should be drawn from this scale.

Measure

Measure directly contributes to readability and pacing of copy. Lines that are too long degrade eye tracking from line to line, while lines that are too short make it difficult to maintain a steady reading rhythm.

Line length: 52-78 characters
