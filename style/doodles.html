Style

Doodles

Doodles live at the core of our brand.

Dave Coverly is Bark's award-winning cartoonist. His doodles are the only approved illustration style for Bark, and they appear on everything we do, from packaging to the walls in our offices.

Usage

While doodles are a way to inject character into our product, they should be used carefully and with meaning, supplementing content rather than distracting from it.

Color

Doodles may be colorized to add emphasis to a particular idea or message. Color should be used thoughtfully, align with the Bark palette, and we never add color to dogs.

Background

When doodles are shown on a colored background, they must be filled in white.

Composition

Doodles can take a good, clean design and make it Bark. They can be used alongside photos, as the centerpiece of a design, or as animations. In all situations, they should be carefully implemented and never distract from more important content.
