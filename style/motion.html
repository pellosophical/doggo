Style

Motion

Motion brings personality, tone, rhythm, and purpose to otherwise static objects.

Dave Coverly is Bark's award-winning cartoonist. His doodles are the only approved illustration style for Bark, and they appear on everything we do, from packaging to the walls in our offices.

Purpose

Motion within an experience should be meaningful and intentional. It is used to draw the user’s attention to essential elements. In order to convey this, the motion needs to be quick, direct, and precise.

Unobtrusive

Motion should be used with discretion—a little bit goes a long way. When applied properly, motion goes unnoticed. Subtlety ensures that the user won’t be distracted and allows for a cohesive experience.

Easing

Strictly linear movement appears strange to the human eye. An animation should accelerate and decelerate smoothly throughout its duration to appear as natural as possible.
